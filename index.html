Justin Russell

Full Stack Engineer, Technology Leader

Justin resides minutes outside the city of Portland on the Southern Coast of Maine. He currently serves as Chief Technology Officer for Club Locker, formerly as Principal Engineer at US Squash and Vice President of Engineering at iVantage Health Analytics.

As an experienced full stack engineer, Justin has a passion for front-end development, system design, enhancing developer experience, mentoring, and tackling difficult problems.

With a background in machine learning and data science, Justin is committed to continuous learning and professional growth. He is a multiple time conference speaker and the founding member of Aupathic, LLC.
