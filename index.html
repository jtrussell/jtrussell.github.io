Project Highlights

✨ GenAI Match Summaries

At Club Locker, we continuously seek new ways to engage users and make the most of our data. By analyzing match histories and telemetry from our real-time scoring app, we generate detailed and insightful match summaries for players.

🚀 Cost Center Coding with NLP

Cost benchmarking relies on standardizing spending categories. At iVantage, we applied natural language processing and document classification techniques to analyze general ledger line items and cost center descriptions, enabling accurate, apples-to-apples comparisons of hospital spending profiles.

🌎 Genetic Algorithms Driven Service Areas

Defining hospital service areas is crucial for market research, outreach, analysis, recruitment, and other initiatives. However, restrictions in certain applications can make these definitions challenging to manage at scale. At iVantage, we leveraged genetic algorithms to gain deeper insights into the hospital service area landscape across the United States. Read more about this use of GA here.
